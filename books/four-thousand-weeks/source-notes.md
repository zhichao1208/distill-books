## Why this book and not adjacent ones

Four Thousand Weeks is the first book in the **finitude / time-philosophy archetype** (new in batch 3). It is structurally a counter-frame to Deep Work and GTD — Burkeman engages GTD by name in Ch. 3 and engages Newport implicitly throughout. The skill exists because the productivity canon (well-represented in this slate by Deep Work, How to Change, Atomic Habits, Make It Stick) needs a counter-frame for users who have done the systems and found them insufficient. Newport's *Slow Productivity* (2024) is the partial reconciliation — the skill routes there for users wanting "now what structurally."
