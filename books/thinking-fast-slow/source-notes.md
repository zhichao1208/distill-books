# Thinking, Fast and Slow · sources

## Book
- *Thinking, Fast and Slow*, Daniel Kahneman, Farrar Straus Giroux, 2011
- ISBN: 978-0374533557 · Chinese: 《思考，快与慢》, 中信出版社, 2012
- Goodreads 4.17 / ~600k ratings · ~10M+ copies · #1 in Psychology
- Kahneman: Nobel Prize 2002 (Economic Sciences for prospect theory); died March 2024

## Chapter mapping (CRITICAL: replication varies by section)

### Survived replication well (skill uses these confidently)
- Part 4 (Ch 25-34): Prospect theory — Ruggeri et al. 2020 Nature Human Behaviour replicated 12 of 13 contrasts across 19 countries
  - Loss aversion: robust
  - Framing effects: robust  
  - Reference dependence: robust
  - Probability weighting: robust
- Ch 11-12: Anchoring — replicated
- Ch 12-13: Availability heuristic — replicated
- Ch 17-18: Regression to mean — sound statistics
- Ch 23: Outside view / planning fallacy — replicated
- Ch 35-36: Peak-end rule + duration neglect — replicated (Redelmeier-Kahneman 1996)
- Ch 24: Engine of Capitalism (optimism) — highest R-Index = 80

### Did NOT survive replication (skill flags or omits)
- **Ch 4: Associative Machine (priming)** — R-Index = 19. Failed: Florida elderly walking, money priming, Lady Macbeth, facial-feedback
- **Ch 3: Ego depletion** — failed Hagger 2016 + Vohs 2021 multi-lab pre-registered replications
- Ch 6, 7, 11, 14, 16: R-Index < 50 (mixed quality)

### Kahneman's own 2017 retraction
February 2017: Kahneman published response on Schimmack's Replicability Index analysis. Quote: "I placed too much faith in underpowered studies." Specifically conceded the priming chapter (Ch 4) was based on research that didn't survive replication. Did NOT retract prospect theory or anchoring chapters.

## Concepts deliberately not used
- Specific priming examples as evidence
- "Ego is depletable" framing
- Some specific bias-magnitude claims that book implies more strongly than research supports
- Facial-feedback hypothesis

## Differentiation from book industry
Most "popular Kahneman" content treats the whole book as equally settled. The skill is honest about which chapters held up.
